Heart Health Tips
Regular Exercise

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week.

Healthy Diet

Consume a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit saturated fats and sodium.

Quit Smoking

Smoking is a major risk factor for heart disease. Seek help to quit if you currently smoke.

Maintain Healthy Weight

Keep your BMI within the healthy range (18.5-24.9) to reduce strain on your heart.

Manage Stress

Practice relaxation techniques like meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises.

Quality Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to support heart health.

Regular Checkups

Schedule regular health screenings to monitor blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar levels.
